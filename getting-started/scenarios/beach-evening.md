# Beach (Evening)

## Introduction

An urban beachfront environment with a distinct Florida feel. There’s plenty of variety to see from boats and piers to patios and hotels, this environment has it all. Train basic flight skills, target captures, search and rescue, or just explore!

The Beach (Evening) scenario consists of 9 modules:
