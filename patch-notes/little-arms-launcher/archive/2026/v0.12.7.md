# ⭐ v0.12.7

### March 13, 2026

New Features:

* **Dedicated DLC view** added for supported apps.
* When you **selectively install, update, or “revert to”** another launcher-app version, a **prompt asks whether DLC should be included**, so simulator build and optional content stay aligned with your intent.

Improvements:

* **DLC catalog list** only shows items that have a defined **latestVersion**, reducing empty or misleading rows.
* **Immediate busy/progress feedback** when installing or updating DLC from app settings (state updates as soon as you click).

Fixes:

* **“Revert to”** now includes the **missing confirmation** path in app controls.
* **More reliable selective updates and DLC handling** in edge cases that previously could error or confuse install state.
* **DLC install/update** hardened when **folders or symlinks** are missing or inconsistent.
