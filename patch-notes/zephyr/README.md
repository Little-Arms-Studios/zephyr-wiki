# Zephyr

## v1.10.7

### December 17, 2025

_This update includes one new scenario, "SAR Mountain", and updates to the existing "SAR Fundamentals" scenario. It also includes an experimental new feature: Return to Home. There are the usual assortment of under-the-hood improvements and bug fixes, including a fix for the drone wobble issue impact systems with lower framerates._

* New Flyable Scenarios

    * This scenario allows user to apply their Search & Rescue training in the large search area of a mountain forest.
    * List of Modules
      * Free Flight - Explore the mountain wilderness at your own pace

      * Lost Climber - A Climber has gone missing and was last seen climbing up the East side of the mountain. Go and search the surrounding areas and use SAR techniques to find the subject as quickly as possible.

* New Flyable Modules
  * SAR Fundamentals
    * List of Modules
      * Detailed Search 01 - There is a lost subject somewhere in the scenario, you will be given a detailed description of them and must find them in the scenario with many people nearby.
      * Orbit Practice 01 - Practice orbiting an object while keeping it in drone camera view.
      * Follow the Trail 01 - Follow the Path Objective points over the trail in the forest while capturing photos of the backpacks below. Then circle back and land safely on the landing pad.
      * Glidescope 02 - Fly the indicated routes while facing the indicated direction.
      * Zoom Reveal 02 - Fly the indicated routes while facing the indicated direction.
      * Missing Camper - Find a missing camper who was last heard from at your landing pad with a plan to camp across the river.
* New Experimental Features
  * Return to Home
    * Users can have the drone automatically return to and land at its home point. The landing spot will not be inch perfect and is intended to be within a few meters. This feature can be activated manually by:
      * Opening the map in a module
      * Selecting "Autonomous"
      * Selecting "Return to Home"
      * Selecting "Start Mission"
