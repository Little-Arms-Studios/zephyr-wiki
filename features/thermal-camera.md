# Thermal Camera

## Overview

Thermal is partially supported throughout the simulator and is still being developed and refined based on pilot feedback and emerging technology.

{% hint style="info" %}
This feature is currently under development and looking for feedback
{% endhint %}

## How to Use Thermal

### Activate Thermal Camera

By default, you can activate thermal by pressing the `T` key on your keyboard. You can change the "Toggle Thermal" button mapping in the [mapping menu.](../getting-started/controller-configuration/#mapping)

### Change Thermal Color Palette

<figure><img src="../.gitbook/assets/Thermal Dropdown.png" alt="Thermal Palette Dropdown"><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

With Thermal active, a dropdown menu will appear. You can select from the following supported thermal color palettes:

* White Hot
* Black Hot
* Red Hot
* Ironbow
* Lava
* Rainbow
* Night Vision
* Graded Fire
* NPFA
